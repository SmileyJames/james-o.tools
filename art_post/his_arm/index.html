His Arm

James O'Toole

Acrylic on canvas

Nov 15 2017

1000mm x 1000mm

The arm grasps a pole marked with chevrons: symbolizing his weapon. The drips of ink from his arm infer blood. However, the depiction of a masculine warrior is undermined by his lactating nipple.
